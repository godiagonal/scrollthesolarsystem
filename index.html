Scroll the Solar System

Welcome to an interactive model of the solar system! Here the laws of physics don't quite apply and you can travel in both space and time.

The overview mode displays our solar system and what it looks like today from a bird's eye view. You can use the slider on the right to make the planets travel back and forth in time. The predictions are based on NASA's own calculations.

To learn more about the major bodies of the solar system, enter the exploration mode and start travelling through space at superluminal velocities.

This site is made purely for fun and learning, combining my interests for programming, astronomy and graphical design. I hope you enjoy and learn some new things about space and all that good stuff!
